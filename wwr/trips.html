Family Float — Easy

Perfect for families and first-time rafters. Gentle rapids and scenic views make this an ideal introduction to the river. Children must be accompanied by an adult.

  • Duration: ~2 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • What's included: Guide, life jacket, helmet, safety briefing
  • Price: $45 / person

Half-Day Whitewater — Intermediate

A more exciting run with several medium rapids, perfect for people who want thrills without committing to a full day. Includes safety briefing and a packed lunch.

  • Duration: ~4 hours
  • Difficulty: Intermediate
  • What's included: Guide, safety gear, packed lunch
  • Price: $95 / person

Full-Day Expedition — Advanced

A full-day adventure including long stretches of rapids, river-side lunch, and optional overnight camping for multi-day expeditions. Recommended for experienced paddlers.

  • Duration: 8+ hours
  • Difficulty: Advanced
  • What's included: Guide, full gear, lunch, optional camping arrangements
  • Price: $180 / person
